About me

I am a former restaurant cook turned software engineer from New Jersey with a penchant for web development. Like cooking, coding excites me in a way that allows me to draw inspiration from people, places, and the captivating stories within them. It allows me to stretch my imagination and, in turn, create a beautiful, cohesive product. I love solving problems and providing elegant, innovative, and accessible solutions. When I'm not coding, you can find me trekking through wondrous landscapes, exploring new cities, and finding hidden gems to dine in.

How Is Your Web Presence?

A web presence is not merely a convenient commodity, it is a vital component to a successful business.

It is my goal to help individuals and small businesses around me to create stunning, accessible, and responsive websites. My design process also encompasses mobile compatability and SEO optimization to help your site stand out. Whether it be starting from scratch, or updating a preexisting site, I want to ensure a quality product is built for my client. Let's see what we can create together!

Process

  • 1. Consultation - Together, we'll discuss the layout that will best achieve your business needs, as well as, enact your vision to its fullest.
  • 2. Proposal - A project proposal will be delivered to you, which includes layout and design mock-ups. We'll work out the fine details to ensure your ideal design is conceptualized.
  • 3. Development - Your website comes to fruition! Look forward to receiving regular updates and check-ins during the entire developmental process.
  • 4. Delivery - Upon completion, the site will launch and all files will be securely delivered to you!
